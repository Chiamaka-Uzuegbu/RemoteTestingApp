App Name: Remote Testing Application

Architecture: Clean Architecture
DataBase: SQL
ORM(Object Relational Mapper): Entity Framework
Framework: ASP.NET Core Web Api
Unit Test: X-Unit
Version Control: Git
Repository: Github
Brief/Module Choosen: Successful Candidate

This brief describes a job applicant who has accessed the company test dashboard for the remote testing, have been allowed to
take the test by the company test Admin and has been declared successful after the results were collated.
They first of all register, or sign-in for one who has registered before. They fill in their personal
details which will be verified. They are subsequently lead to review and sign company documents that will
be presented to them at this stage. They will be required to upload their certificates which will be an Ifile.
After this, they can now access the workspace and dashboard.
